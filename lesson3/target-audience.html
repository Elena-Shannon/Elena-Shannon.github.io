Target audience and Scenarios

Description: The main target audience would be both men and women 20-50 years old who enjoy and spend a lot of time outdoors either alone or with their famileis for recreational or work-related reasons.

Persona 1: Tom is a farmer currently living in Preston, Idaho. He owns a small farm (about 150 acres) where he grows potatoes and various fall crops, animal feed, and other consumer products. He relies on accurate weather information to plan the time to pick new crops, to winterwize the land, to calculate gains and losses, and to prepare for the future.

Persona 2: Alex is a physically active single young man who attends Idaho State University, works part-time doing landscaping, and enjoys time with his friends and family.


Scenario: 1.Tom plans to spend all Saturday harvesting potatoes. He wants to know if the weather will be good over the weekend.

Scenario: 2. There is a fall festival coming up in Preston, ID, starting on Friday. Tom entered his 1200-pound pumpkin into a Giant Pumpkin Competition, but he heard from the neighbors that a big storm is coming in. He is not sure if he will be able to transport his pumpkin to the location.

Scenario: 3. Alex's Grandma lives in Preston, ID. During the break, Alex wants to go visit her. He wants to know what the weather will be like and if there are any interesting events coming up in the area.

Scenario: 4. Alex's cousin Mike is visiting from UT in two weeks. Alex wants to go to Bear Lake in Fish Haven to water ski and paddle board with his cousin and a few of his other friends. He needs to know if it will be sunny on their weekend away.

Scenario: 5. The company Alex works for wants him to install sod at a house in Soda Springs, ID. Alex wants to know what the weather will be like as he works on scheduling the best day that would work for him and the family he will be working for.
